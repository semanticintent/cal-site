# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date**: January 9, 2026
**Last Updated**: January 9, 2026

## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how the CAL Documentation website uses cookies and similar technologies.

**TL;DR**: We currently use **zero cookies**. This page exists for transparency and future reference.

---

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are used to:
- Remember preferences (theme, language)
- Track analytics (page views, user behavior)
- Enable functionality (authentication, shopping carts)

---

## Cookies We Use

### Current Status: **No Cookies**

We currently do **not** use any cookies, including:

- ❌ **Analytics cookies** - No Google Analytics, no tracking
- ❌ **Advertising cookies** - No ads, no retargeting
- ❌ **Third-party cookies** - No external tracking
- ❌ **Authentication cookies** - No login required

### Local Storage (Browser-Side Only)

VitePress may use **localStorage** for:
- **Theme preference** (light/dark mode toggle)
- **Search index** (local search functionality)

This data:
- ✅ Never leaves your browser
- ✅ Never sent to any server
- ✅ Can be cleared via browser settings

## Your Choices

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies via your browser:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- **Firefox**: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

### Clear Local Storage

To clear VitePress local storage:

1. Open browser DevTools (F12)
2. Go to "Application" or "Storage" tab
3. Find "Local Storage"
4. Delete entries for this site
