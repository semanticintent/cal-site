# 6D Foraging Methodology

## Overview

The 6D Foraging Methodology maps business impact across six organizational dimensions, scored through a 3D lens (Sound × Space × Time).

## Origin

Derived from the Cormorant Foraging Framework:

- **Cormorant behavior** → Intelligence patterns
- **3 mediums** (air, land, water) → 3D Lens
- **Hunting strategy** → Analysis methodology

## The 3D Lens

| Dimension | Cormorant | Business |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **Sound** | Acoustic detection | Urgency/intensity |
| **Space** | Spatial positioning | Scope/reach |
| **Time** | Temporal memory | Velocity/change |

## Scoring

```
Dimension Score = (Sound × Space × Time) / 10
```

Score ranges 0.1 to 100.

## Cascade Probability

| Score | Level | Probability |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| ≥ 70 | Critical | 90% |
| ≥ 50 | High | 70% |
| ≥ 30 | Medium | 50% |
| ≥ 15 | Low | 30% |
| < 15 | Minimal | 10% |

## Multiplier Effect

| Dimensions × Depth | Multiplier |
|--------------------|------------|
| ≥ 15 | 10-15× |
| ≥ 10 | 6-10× |
| ≥ 6 | 4-6× |
| ≥ 3 | 2-4× |
| < 3 | 1.5-2× |

## The Six Dimensions

### D1: Customer (External Stakeholders)
**Impact on people who buy from you**

- Churn risk
- NPS decline
- Support volume
- Trust erosion

**Example:** Customer complaints increase, renewals at risk

### D2: Employee (Internal People)
**Impact on your workforce**

- Attrition
- Engagement drops
- Knowledge loss
- Morale issues

**Example:** Key engineers leave, tribal knowledge gone

### D3: Revenue (Financial Impact)
**Direct money effects**

- Revenue decline
- Cost increases
- Deal slippage
- Margin compression

**Example:** ARR drops, burn rate increases

### D4: Regulatory (Compliance)
**Legal and regulatory risk**

- Audit findings
- Violations
- Fines
- License risk

**Example:** Data privacy breach, SOC 2 failure

### D5: Quality (Product/Service)
**What you deliver**

- Defects
- Technical debt
- Feature delays
- Performance issues

**Example:** Bug count rises, releases delayed

### D6: Operational (Processes/Systems)
**How you operate**

- System failures
- Process breaks
- Infrastructure issues
- Tool problems

**Example:** CI/CD breaks, deployment frequency drops

## Real Example: Tailwind CSS (UC-002)

### The Situation
- 75% workforce reduction (9 → 2 people)
- AI tool broke marketing funnel
- Revenue dropped 80%
- Direct cost: ~$300K

### 6D Analysis

| Dimension | Score | Finding |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| D1 Customer | 21.0 | Trust damaged, competitors emerged |
| D2 Employee | 57.6 | 75% layoffs, knowledge loss |
| D3 Revenue | 64.8 | 80% decline, sustainability risk |
| D4 Regulatory | 0.0 | Not affected |
| D5 Quality | 21.0 | Skeleton crew, innovation risk |
| D6 Operational | 57.6 | AI broke funnel, processes fragile |

### Cascade Calculation

- **Dimensions affected:** 5 (D1, D2, D3, D5, D6)
- **Depth:** 2 levels
- **Multiplier:** 6-10× (severe)

**Result:**
- Direct cost: $300K
- Cascade impact: $2.1M - $3.4M
- Hidden cost: 7-11× the visible cost

## Common Cascade Pathways

### Operations → People → Quality → Customer → Revenue
```
D6 → D2 → D5 → D1 → D3

Example:
CI/CD breaks → Engineers frustrated → Bugs increase →
Customers churn → Revenue drops
```

### Revenue → People → Operations
```
D3 → D2 → D6

Example:
Revenue pressure → Layoffs → Operational gaps → More pressure
```

### People → Quality → Customer
```
D2 → D5 → D1

Example:
Knowledge loss → Quality drops → Customer impact
```

## Using 6D in CAL

```cal
-- Full 6D analysis
FORAGE entities
WHERE sound > 7
ACROSS D1, D2, D3, D4, D5, D6
DEPTH 3
SURFACE complete_cascade

-- Focus on specific dimensions
FORAGE situations
WHERE segment = "enterprise"
ACROSS D1, D2, D3  -- Customer, Employee, Revenue
DEPTH 2
SURFACE critical_path
```

## Cascade Depth

| Depth | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Direct impact only | Bug affects one feature |
| 2 | One level of cascade | Bug → Customer complaint → Support load |
| 3 | Two cascade levels | Bug → Complaint → Support → Engineering time |
| 4+ | Deep cascade | Full organizational impact chain |

**Recommendation:** Start with depth 2-3 for most analyses.

## Assessment Framework

### Step 1: Score Each Dimension
Use the 3D lens (Sound × Space × Time) / 10

### Step 2: Calculate Multiplier
Dimensions affected × Depth = Multiplier lookup

### Step 3: Estimate Impact
Direct cost × Multiplier = Total cascade impact

### Step 4: Decide Action
Use DRIFT + Fetch to determine response
