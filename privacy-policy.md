# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 9, 2026
**Last Updated**: January 9, 2026

## Introduction

CAL Documentation ("we", "us", or "our") operates this documentation website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

**TL;DR**: We don't collect personal data, track you, or use cookies. Your privacy is respected by design.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

We do **not** require you to create an account or provide personal information to access CAL documentation.

### Automatically Collected Information

**Currently Collected**: None

- No analytics tracking
- No cookies or local storage (beyond temporary session data)
- No IP address logging
- No user behavior tracking

**VitePress Local Search**: Our documentation uses VitePress's built-in local search, which indexes content **entirely in your browser**. Search data never leaves your device.

### Future Changes

If we implement analytics or tracking in the future:
- This policy will be updated with clear notice
- We will use privacy-respecting tools (e.g., Plausible Analytics, Cloudflare Analytics)
- You will be notified via this page
